Sea Turtle Preservation Society

STPS Mission Statement says it all: "Helping Sea Turtles Survive. STPS volunteers are a dedicated bunch!" With only one employee, who works the Sea Turtle House, donations go towards saving the turtles. Volunteers support education, rescues, monthly beach clean-up and so much more. The majority of our funds come from our annual fundraiser, Turtle Krawl 5K. Our virtual race supports this event.
